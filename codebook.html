Mean of Measurement values

The mean of a series of variables corresponding to each subject and each subject activity were calulated based on the original aggregated data.

Some variables would also have a X,Y,Z term to indicate information for each of the three dimensions. The terms may also have a term mean() to indicate that it is the mean of the raw data and std() to indicate that it is the standard deviation.

All the variables have a suffix of “.Subject.Activity.mean” to indicate that there are calculated means of the corresponding variable for each subject and each activity.
